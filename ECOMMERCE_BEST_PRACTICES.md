## Conversion Optimization Techniques Applied

### 1. **F-Pattern Layout**
Content arranged for natural eye movement (left to right, top to bottom).

### 2. **Visual Hierarchy**
- Largest: Hero headline and price
- Medium: Section headers and CTAs
- Small: Body text and details

### 3. **Color Psychology**
- **Blue**: Trust, professionalism, calm
- **Green**: Success, safety, go-ahead
- **Orange/Yellow**: Urgency, attention
- **White**: Clean, simple, modern

### 4. **Contrast & Readability**
- High contrast CTAs (blue on white)
- Readable text sizes (16px minimum)
- Proper line spacing (1.6 line-height)

### 5. **Progressive Disclosure**
Information revealed in logical order:
1. Problem (ronquidos)
2. Solution (DeepSleep)
3. Benefits (mejor sueÃ±o)
4. Proof (testimonials)
5. Guarantee (risk reversal)
